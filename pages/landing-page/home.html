FAQ

Questions clients ask before starting

How do I know the right paint type for my building?

We assess surface condition, project location, moisture levels, and usage needs before recommending the correct paint system.

Do you only supply paint or handle project execution too?

We support both. You can order products only, or request full advisory and application coordination for your site team.

How long does an exterior system typically last?

Depending on exposure and maintenance quality, our recommended exterior systems are designed for multi-year protection and reduced repaint cycles.

Can I get a quote before site inspection?

Yes. We provide a preliminary estimate from your measurements, then finalize pricing after material and surface verification.
